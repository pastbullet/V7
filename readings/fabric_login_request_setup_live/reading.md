Section 6.2.2.2 defines the Explicit Fabric Login Request setup. It states that the Nx_Port sends FLOGI in a new Exchange and that the FLOGI payload carries the Nx_Port Service Parameters, a 64-bit N_Port_Name, and a 64-bit Node_Name. It further states that these Service Parameters are specified for F_Port Login in 6.6 and that their Fabric Login applicability is given in tables 150 and 155. The request setup also requires the Nx_Port to assign an OX_ID and set D_ID to the well-known F_Port address FFFFFEh. For an unidentified Nx_Port, S_ID shall be set either to 000000h or to 0000h || YY. The text explains that 000000h requests Fabric assignment of all 24 bits of the N_Port_ID, while 0000h || YY requests Fabric assignment of the upper 16 bits and validation of the lower 8 bits. Separately, 6.2.1 prohibits attempting communication with other Nx_Ports until Fabric Login is complete. Table 150 and Table 155 are the cited dependency tables for FLOGI Service Parameter applicability; they include FLOGI and FLOGI LS_ACC applicability columns.
