Relevant text is in §6.2.2.5 "Relogin with the Fabric" on page 155 and §6.2.3 "SOFs" on pages 155-156, within the Explicit Fabric Login area. The relogin paragraph states that if a new Fabric Login yields the same N_Port_ID, F_Port_Name, and Fabric_Name as the previous login, current communications may continue; if any of those values changed, the Nx_Port shall implicitly logout with all Nx_Ports, wait an R_A_TOV timeout beginning when the change is detected, and then perform new N_Port Logins before communicating with other Nx_Ports. The SOF paragraph states that Fabric Login is only performed in Class 1, 2, or 3; login is valid for all supported classes indicated by FLOGI LS_ACC validity bits; SOFc1 may be used for Fabric Login or Fabric Relogin while SOFi1 is not allowed; Class 6 shall not be used for Fabric Login; SOF selection for FLOGI is based on classes supported by the originating Nx_Port; after an F_RJT with class-of-service-not-supported at FFFFFEh, another supported SOF shall be attempted; and if all supported SOF types are attempted and all are rejected or time out, the Fabric and Nx_Port are incompatible and outside intervention is required.
